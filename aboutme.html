Hey There!

I'm Caroline
a passionate French Graphic Designer & Creative with over a decade of experience.

When I was 2-year-old in my grandparents' house, I was taking a little nap. But instead of sleeping, I decided it was the perfect time to embark on my artistic journey.

What did I do? I ripped off the old wallpaper. It was a tiny piece of paper just begging for a makeover, and I couldn't resist. It was my first-ever design project, and it set the stage for my creative adventures.

Design isn't just my job;
it's my lifelong love affair.
